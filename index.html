WELCOME TO THE ULTIMATE CANYONERO SHRINE!

Greetings fellow sandwich enthusiasts! My name is Mike_SandwichLover94 and I am ON A MISSION to bring back the legendary CANYONERO SANDWICH!

For those who don't know, the Canyonero was named after the mighty SUV from The Simpsons (Season 9, Episode 15 - "The Last Temptation of Krust"). Just like its automotive namesake, this sandwich was ENORMOUS, POWERFUL, and absolutely UNSTOPPABLE!
